Save the World Basics

Welcome to Fortnite: Save the World! Save the World (StW for short) is an early access, co-op, horde survival shooter game. It has been in development since 2011. Since its release, it has sold millions of copies.

The basic gameplay is similar to a tower defense, where your character (hero) has to defend an objective against waves of zombie-like creatures (Husks) and powerful mini-bosses (Mist Monsters). When not in a mission, you can level up and become more powerful.

In this website, you will learn all about the interesting and complex game of Save the World. There will be walkthroughs for husk pathing, build guides, tips for becoming more powerful, and simple game etiquette.
